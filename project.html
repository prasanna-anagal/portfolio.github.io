Project Details

The problem space focuses on optimizing urban mobility and infrastructure in growing cities. Key findings include:
  • Inefficient traffic patterns due to outdated signaling systems.
  • Underutilization of public transit due to poor accessibility and scheduling.
  • High carbon emissions caused by poorly optimized logistics networks.
Smart city designs incorporating IoT sensors, AI-driven traffic management, and multimodal transportation systems were explored.

Problem Definition:

Our project focuses on improving urban mobility through real-time traffic management, optimized public transit, and sustainable logistics.
