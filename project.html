Project Details

Exploring innovative solutions for urban mobility and sustainability.

Explore Project Insights

The problem space focuses on optimizing urban mobility and infrastructure in growing cities. It involves analyzing inefficiencies in traffic flow, public transportation, and the environmental impact of city logistics.

  • Inefficient traffic patterns due to outdated signaling systems.
  • Underutilization of public transit due to poor accessibility and scheduling.
  • High carbon emissions caused by poorly optimized logistics networks.

Problem Definition:

Designing a data-driven solution to improve urban mobility by integrating real-time traffic management.
