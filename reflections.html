Learning Reflections

Through this course, we explored data structures like trees, heaps, and graphs, and algorithms such as backtracking and dynamic programming. Here's a breakdown of key takeaways:

  • Efficiency: The importance of space and time complexity.
  • Design Principles: Understanding iterative and recursive approaches.
  • Applications: Using spanning trees for connectivity and hashing for quick lookups.
